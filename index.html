Location

Povljana, Island of Pag

Povljana is a charming village on the southern tip of the island of Pag, known for its calm, shallow waters ideal for families and crystal-clear sea. The island is easily reachable by ferry and offers a unique blend of untouched nature, local gastronomy, and authentic Croatian island life.
